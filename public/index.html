Welcome to Loyola ICT Center

Located in the heart of Wau, South Sudan, Loyola ICT Center is a leading provider of computer education and training. Our experienced instructors offer hands-on, practical training in a variety of courses. With flexible schedules and affordable tuition, we make it easy for students to learn new skills and advance their careers. Contact us today to learn more and join our growing community of successful graduates.

Why Choose Loyola ICT Center?

Companies across the globe will continue to face technology talent gaps over the next decade. ICT professionals are some of the most in demand, higher paid professionals helping to navigate the digital trasnsformation taking place across the business landscape. Our programmes prepare you with the technical and work ready skills for a global career as a Computer Professional. Loyola ICT Center was established with the aim of transforming lives through providing affordable short courses to all. We ensure learning is practical, fun and relevant to learners by bringing real life issues to the classroom. We aim at equipping our trainees with the skills they need now, to work now, earn now and forever.

How to apply

Application forms may be obtained from our Offices at Loyola Secondary School - Wau, at a fee of SSP. 2,000. The completed forms, together with photocopy copy of valid ID and a 2 recent passport size photos, must be hand delivered to the Registrar at Loyoal ICT Center offices.
